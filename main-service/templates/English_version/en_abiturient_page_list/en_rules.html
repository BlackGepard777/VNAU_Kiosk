General Provisions

1. The rules of admission to Vinnytsia National Agrarian University for higher education studies in 2025 (hereinafter - Admission Rules) were developed by the Admissions Committee of Vinnytsia National Agrarian University (hereinafter - Admissions Committee) in accordance with the Procedure for Admission to Higher Education Studies in 2025, approved by Order of the Ministry of Education and Science of Ukraine dated February 10, 2025, No. 168, and registered with the Ministry of Justice of Ukraine on February 26, 2025, No. 15/41360. Changes to the admission rules were made in accordance with the Order of the Ministry of Education and Science of Ukraine dated June 18, 2025, No. 877, registered with the Ministry of Justice of Ukraine on June 25, 2025, No. 987/44393 "On the Approval of Changes to the Procedure for Admission to Higher Education Studies in 2025 with amendments and additions introduced by the Order of the Ministry of Education and Science of Ukraine dated June 27, 2025, No. 936."

2. The basis for announcing admission is the licenses of the Ministry of Education and Science of Ukraine for educational activities at the higher education level(s), for educational programs that provide for the award of a professional qualification in professions for which additional regulation has been introduced at a certain level of higher education, and the admission rules, which are approved in accordance with the law, the Procedure, and these Admission Rules.

3. Admission to study is carried out by the university on a competitive basis.

4. The organization of applicant admission is carried out by the admissions committee - a working body of the university. The admissions committee acts in accordance with the regulations on the admissions committee of Vinnytsia National Agrarian University, developed taking into account the requirements of the law, the Regulations on the admissions committee of a higher educational institution, approved by the Order of the Ministry of Education and Science of Ukraine dated October 15, 2015, No. 1085, registered with the Ministry of Justice of Ukraine on November 04, 2015, No. 1353/27798. The regulations on the admissions committee of the Vinnytsia National Agrarian University are published on its official website. The Rector of the university ensures compliance with the legislation of Ukraine, in particular the Admission Rules and the Procedure, as well as the openness and transparency of the admissions committee's work. The decision of the admissions committee, made within its competence, is the basis for issuing a corresponding order and/or performing the procedures of the admission campaign. All issues related to admission to study are resolved by the admissions committee at its meetings. The decisions of the admissions committee are published on the official website of the university no later than the next day after their adoption.

Evaluation Criteria for the Motivation Letter

1. Adherence to the requirements of the official business style.

2. Structure and logical arrangement of semantic blocks.

3. Literacy.

4. Combination of conciseness and informative presentation.

5. Ability to define a goal, present oneself, and one's individuality.

6. Level of motivation to enroll in Vinnytsia National Agrarian University for the corresponding specialty.
